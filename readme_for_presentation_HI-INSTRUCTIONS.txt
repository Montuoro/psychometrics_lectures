Rasch Model Lecture Series
===========================

LECTURES
--------
1. Lecture_1_Fundamental_Measurement.html - Rasch measurement, Guttman structures, invariance, latent traits

OPENING A LECTURE
------------------
1. Open a terminal in this folder:
   C:\Data Projects\html presentations\rasch model series
2. Run: npx serve . -p 5555
3. Open in your browser: http://localhost:5555/Lecture_1_Fundamental_Measurement.html

CONTROLS
--------
- Arrow Right / Arrow Left: Navigate between slides
- Click "Next" / "Previous" buttons at the bottom
- Press P: Open presenter notes in a separate window

PRESENTER NOTES
---------------
Press P or click "Presenter (P)" in the bottom bar to open the presenter view.
Keep this window on a secondary screen while presenting.

INTERACTIVE SLIDES (Lecture 1)
-------------------------------
- Slide 5 (Guttman Structure): Use the Slow/Medium/Fast buttons to animate the person-by-item buildup. Reset to start over.
- Slide 6 (Invariance): Toggle between All 20 Items, Odd Items Only, and Even Items Only to demonstrate invariance.
- Slide 9 (Order, Probability, and Misfit): Switch between Deterministic, Probabilistic Noise, and Misfit views.

STOPPING THE SERVER
-------------------
Close the terminal window, or press Ctrl+C in the terminal.
